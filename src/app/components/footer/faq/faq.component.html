FAQ
Your Questions Answered

  1. About the artists and their work
    • How do you choose the artworks available for sale?

      We select our artists, and they choose the works they want to sell. Nevertheless, we do moderate the content and it is possible that we may remove a work if its authenticity is questioned.

    • Who can sell their work on ArtGallery?

      All of our artists are well established in their country of residence. We identify these artists across the globe so we can offer you a diverse, high-quality international selection.

    • Are the artworks available as prints?

      All the works on ArtGallery are original artworks; they are not reproduced and further editions are not published by the artist.

  2. Services offered by ArtGallery
    • Is framing available for all works?

      Absolutely. Sometimes the artist has already chosen a frame, which we can send you a photo of.

    • Who is responsible for the cost of framing?

      When the artwork is already framed, then the frame is a part of the work and therefore included in the price.

    • Do you offer recommendations and advice?

      We can offer you a personalised selection of about twenty works which are chosen especially for you.

  3. Buying art with ArtGallery
    • How do I find out if you have artworks by the artist I'm looking for?

      If you have an artist name in mind, you can search for them on our artists page.

    • What does the heart next to artworks do?

      This symbol allows you to choose the artworks you love while you're on the site, so you can find them all on one single page.
