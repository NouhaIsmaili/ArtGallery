About Raffin Christian

Raffin Christian is a French artist born in 1957 in Isère, the French alps. When he was only a young teenager, he became passionate about art and more particularly for drawing thanks to one of his teachers in college.

For sometime he put this passion aside in order to dedicate to his studies in engineering.

Once he graduated, he installed himself in Tunisia from 1982 to 1984 where he practiced photography and tried painting, imbuing himself in the colors of the country.
